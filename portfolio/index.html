About me

I took the scenic route.

Throughout my 20's my entire world was music ... from recording and performing around San Diego, to music videos and touring around the US. I quickly learned that although being on stage was thrilling, what really fueled my passion was the creative process. As this evolved to include web design and digital marketing, I was recruited to help national lifestyle and wellness brands expand their online presence.

I loved all of it, but wanted to do more than make logos shine and readerships grow. I wanted to reach deeper under the hood, peek further behind the curtain. In the past 10+ years a new world of web and mobile apps has emerged, changing the way we live in more ways than we can measure. As a Full Stack Web Developer with an instinct for the 'human' side of the equation, my obsession with digital creation grows ever deeper by the day.
